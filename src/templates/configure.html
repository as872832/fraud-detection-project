Configuration Guide
Strict

Best for: High-risk environments, new customers, or when fraud cost is very high.
Trade-off: Catches more fraud but generates more false alarms.

Moderate

Best for: Standard operations with established customers.
Trade-off: Balanced approach - good fraud detection with reasonable false alarm rate.

Lenient

Best for: VIP customers, business accounts, or when false alarms are costly.
Trade-off: Fewer false alarms but may miss some fraud.
